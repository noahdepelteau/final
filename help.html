what's going on?

the changes you've been experiencing as you navigate this site, and these memories, represent the changes a brain experiencing dementia can ungergo. This website intends to foster awareness of the condition, and breed compassion for this experiencing dementia.

dementia is an umbrella term describing the impairement of memory, thinking, and decision making, to the extent that everyday life is disturbed. it commonly effects older adults but is not apart of normal aging. dementia is progressive, meaning it becomes more sever with time. as the brain continues to change, it may reach a threshold beyond which there is no cognitive return, and brain function is permanently lost. as such, catching it before this threshold is essential to preserving brain function.
